## Key Assumptions

Based on Pricing v2.0 tiers (Starter $2,500+$699/mo, Professional $5,000+$1,499/mo, Enterprise $10,000+$2,499/mo) with expected client mix of 50% Starter / 35% Professional / 15% Enterprise.

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Weighted average setup fee | $4,500 | 50% × $2,500 + 35% × $5,000 + 15% × $10,000 |
| Weighted average MRR per client | $1,249 | 50% × $699 + 35% × $1,499 + 15% × $2,499 |
| Average add-on revenue per client | $150/mo | ~30% of clients take 1 add-on |
| Effective MRR per client | $1,399 | Base MRR + add-on average |
| Monthly churn rate (conservative) | 6% |
| Monthly churn rate (realistic) | 5% |
| Monthly churn rate (aggressive) | 4% |
| Average variable cost per client/month | $180 | Retell, Claude API, hosting, monitoring |

> **Note:** v1.0 used $8,000 avg setup and $2,000 avg MRR based on earlier pricing estimates. v2.0 reflects finalised tier pricing and realistic client mix. Revenue projections are more conservative but margins remain strong at 78–86%.

## 8. Cash Flow Considerations

### Cash Flow Timing

Setup fees are collected upfront (50% on signing, 50% on delivery). Monthly recurring is billed on the 1st of each month. This means cash flow is front-loaded relative to delivery.

### Recommended Cash Reserves

| Milestone | Recommended Reserve | Rationale |
|-----------|-------------------|-----------|
| Month 1–3 | $5,000 | Cover tool costs and initial marketing spend |
| Month 4–6 | $10,000 | Buffer for slower months, tool upgrades |
| Month 7–9 | $20,000 | Pre-holiday cash buffer |
| Month 10–12 | $30,000 | Runway for first hire, if applicable |

### Annual Prepayment Option

Offer clients a 10% discount for paying annually upfront. If 20% of clients take this option:

| Scenario (Realistic) | Annual prepay revenue (20% of 30 clients) | Discount cost | Net benefit |
|----------------------|------------------------------------------|---------------|-------------|
| 6 clients pay annually | 6 × $16,788 × 0.9 = $90,655 upfront | $10,073 discount | Strong cash flow boost for hiring/investment |
