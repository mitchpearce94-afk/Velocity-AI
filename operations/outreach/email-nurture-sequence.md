## Sequence Notes

**Sending platform:** Use your email automation tool (e.g., Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, or built-in CRM automation).

**Personalisation:** At minimum, use [First Name]. Where possible, reference their trade, location, or the context of how they entered the sequence (LinkedIn conversation, website download, referral, etc.).

**Opt-out:** Every email must include an unsubscribe link. Australian Spam Act compliance is non-negotiable.

**Tracking:** Monitor open rates, click rates, and reply rates for each email. Target benchmarks: 35%+ open rate, 5%+ click rate for the CTA emails.

**After the sequence:** If they haven't booked a call, move them to a monthly newsletter or long-term nurture track. Don't keep hammering them with the same sequence.

**If they reply:** Stop the sequence immediately and respond personally. A reply is a conversation, not a trigger for the next automated email.
