# Case Study: Centrefit Group

## From Spreadsheet Chaos to Automated Operations in 2 Weeks

**Industry:** Security & Technology Installation

**Previous System:** Tradify + manual processes
**Solution:** Custom AI-powered CRM & operations platform by Velocity AI

---

## The Challenge

Centrefit Group was growing fast — but their systems weren't keeping up.

Like most trade businesses, they'd started with the basics. Tradify for job management, spreadsheets for everything else, and a whole lot of manual effort to keep the wheels turning. It worked when they were small. But as the team grew, the cracks became impossible to ignore.

**Quoting was a bottleneck.** With 287 products across multiple categories, putting a quote together meant flicking between spreadsheets, checking pricing, manually building line items, and formatting PDFs. A single quote could take hours. And when you're quoting multiple jobs a day, that's days of the week gone — just on paperwork.

**Scheduling was reactive, not proactive.** Jobs were assigned through phone calls and messages. Changes meant a chain of texts. Double-bookings happened. Jobs fell through the gaps. The team was spending more time coordinating than actually doing the work.

**There was no single source of truth.** Customer information lived in Tradify. Pricing lived in spreadsheets. Job notes lived in text messages. Status updates lived in people's heads. Getting a clear picture of the business at any given moment meant asking five different people and hoping the answers matched.

**Double-entry was killing productivity.** The same information was being typed into multiple systems — once in Tradify, again in spreadsheets, again in emails. Every time data was re-entered, it introduced errors and wasted time.

The reality was clear: Centrefit Group didn't have an operations system. They had a collection of workarounds held together by manual effort and good intentions.

---

## The Solution

Velocity AI built Centrefit Group a custom operations platform from the ground up — designed around exactly how they work.

### Custom CRM with Full Product Catalogue

A purpose-built CRM housing all 287 products, complete with pricing, specifications, and categorisation. No more spreadsheet lookups. No more outdated price lists floating around. One system, one source of truth.

### 6-Step Quote Wizard

The centrepiece of the build. A guided quoting flow that walks anyone on the team through creating a professional, accurate quote in minutes:

1. **Select the customer** — auto-populated from the CRM, or create a new one on the spot.
2. **Choose the job type** — the system filters relevant products and pricing automatically.
3. **Build the quote** — drag, drop, and select from the product catalogue. Quantities, pricing, and margins calculated in real time.
4. **Add custom items** — for bespoke work that doesn't fit the standard catalogue.
5. **Review and adjust** — see the full quote, margin, and total before it goes out.
6. **Send** — professional branded PDF generated and emailed to the customer instantly. One click.

What used to take hours now takes minutes. And because the system enforces consistent pricing and formatting, every quote that goes out is accurate and professional.

### Plan Builder

For larger, multi-phase projects, the plan builder lets the team map out entire jobs — stages, timelines, resource allocation — all within the same system. No separate project management tool. No additional subscriptions. It's all connected.

### Auto-Status System

Jobs move through statuses automatically based on real actions — quote sent, quote accepted, job scheduled, job in progress, job completed, invoice sent, paid. No one needs to manually update a whiteboard or drag a card across a Trello board. The system reflects reality in real time.

### Centralised Dashboard

A live view of the entire business. Quotes out, jobs in progress, revenue this month, team utilisation, follow-ups due. The kind of visibility that used to require half a day of pulling reports now loads in seconds.

## The Results

The impact was immediate and measurable.

**Quote generation went from hours to minutes.** The 6-step wizard eliminated the spreadsheet dance entirely. The team can now produce accurate, branded quotes on-site or in the office — without needing Mitchell to be involved in every single one.

**Centralised operations replaced scattered workarounds.** One system for customers, products, quotes, jobs, and reporting. No more Tradify for this, spreadsheet for that, text message for the other thing.

**Double-entry was eliminated.** Data entered once flows through the entire system — from quote to job to invoice. No re-keying. No mismatches. No wasted time.

**The team became self-sufficient.** Because the system is intuitive and trades-focused, the team adopted it quickly. They didn't need to be "techy" — they just needed to follow the steps.

**Decision-making got faster.** With real-time dashboards and automated status tracking, Centrefit Group's leadership can see exactly where the business stands at any moment — and make decisions based on data, not gut feel.
